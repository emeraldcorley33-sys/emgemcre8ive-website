Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 12-08-2025

Your privacy matters. This policy explains how emgemcre8ive collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

We collect information you provide directly when you:

  • Place an order or inquire about services
  • Subscribe to newsletters or download free printables
  • Contact us via email or forms

Personal Information May Include:

  • Contact Details: Name, email address, phone number (if provided)
  • Order Details: Personalization requests, design preferences, event details
  • Payment Information: Payment method selected (Zelle, CashApp, Venmo, PayPal). Note: We do not store payment credentials—all transactions are processed through the selected payment platform's secure systems.
  • Communication Records: Messages exchanged regarding orders, inquiries, or support requests

We use your information to:

  • Fulfill Orders: Create, customize, and deliver your digital or physical products
  • Communicate: Send order confirmations, shipping updates, and respond to inquiries
  • Improve Services: Understand customer needs and enhance our offerings
  • Marketing (Optional): Send newsletters, promotions, or free printable announcements if you've opted in. You can unsubscribe anytime.

We will never sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

Accepted Payment Methods

We accept payments through the following platforms:

  • Zelle: Direct bank transfer
  • CashApp: Mobile payment service
  • Venmo: Mobile payment service
  • PayPal: Secure online payment platform

Important: Each payment platform has its own privacy policy and terms of service. When you complete a transaction, your payment details are processed directly by the chosen platform—emgemcre8ive does not store credit card numbers, bank account details, or other sensitive payment credentials.

We only retain transaction confirmation details (date, amount, order ID) for record-keeping and customer service purposes.

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. This includes:

  • Secure email communication for order details and personalization
  • Password-protected devices and accounts
  • Limited access to personal data (only accessible by emgemcre8ive for order fulfillment)

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. Please use caution when sharing sensitive details online.

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties, except in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Trusted partners who assist with shipping (USPS), email delivery, or file hosting (e.g., Canva for template access). These providers only access information necessary to perform their services.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, court order, or government regulation, we may disclose information to comply with legal processes.
  • Business Transfer: In the event of a business sale or merger, customer information may be transferred as part of the transaction. You'll be notified if this occurs.

Our website does not currently use cookies or tracking technologies. If this changes in the future, we'll update this policy and notify you accordingly.

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: Ask us to update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal or contractual obligations)
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time via the unsubscribe link or by contacting us

To exercise any of these rights, email us at emgemcre8ive@yahoo.com with "Privacy Request" in the subject line.

Our services are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately and we'll delete it.

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill orders, provide customer support, and comply with legal obligations. Typical retention periods:

  • Order Records: Kept for up to 7 years for accounting and tax purposes
  • Email Communications: Retained as long as needed for customer service or until you request deletion
  • Marketing Lists: Retained until you unsubscribe

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. When we make significant changes, we'll update the "Effective Date" at the top of this page and may notify you via email.

Your continued use of our services after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your information, please contact us:

  • Email: emgemcre8ive@yahoo.com
  • Business Name: emgemcre8ive

We'll respond to your inquiry within 3 business days.
