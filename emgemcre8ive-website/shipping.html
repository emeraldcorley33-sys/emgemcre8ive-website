Shipping & Return Policy

Effective Date: 12-08-2025

Covers party favors, printed keepsakes, signage, and all other physical products. Digital items deliver instantly via email.

Processing Time

All physical products are made to order. Please allow 3–7 business days for processing and creation. Larger orders or peak seasons may require extra time (I'll confirm if so).

Shipping Methods

  • Standard (Default): USPS Ground Advantage — estimated 2–5 business days after processing.
  • Upgraded: USPS Priority Mail — estimated 1–3 business days after processing (available at checkout for an additional fee).

Shipping timeframes are carrier estimates and not guaranteed.

Shipping Confirmation

You'll receive an email with tracking as soon as your order ships.

Delays & Lost Packages

Once handed to USPS, carrier delays, lost, or stolen packages are outside my control. If a package is marked delivered but missing, please contact USPS to file a claim. I'll help with documentation if needed.

Digital Items

Digital downloads and Canva links are delivered instantly to your inbox—no shipping required.

Personalization

All custom physical items (party favors, printed keepsakes, signage, merch) are made-to-order. Please double-check names, dates, colors, and themes before purchasing.

Changes & Cancellations

You may request changes or cancel within 12 hours of purchase. After that, production may have begun and changes are not guaranteed.

Final Sale

Custom and personalized items are final sale. Digital items are non-refundable once delivered.

Damaged or Incorrect Items

If your item arrives damaged or incorrect, contact me using the form below within 3 business days.

  • Include: order number, a clear photo of the issue, and a brief description.

If the issue was carrier damage or my error, I'll either replace at no cost, offer a refund (full or partial based on the issue), or request a return (return shipping covered if required). Please keep packaging until it's resolved.

Questions?

I'm here to help.

Use the contact form below for shipping, delivery, or order issues.
