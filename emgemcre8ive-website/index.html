FAQs

Questions? I've got answers.

  • Turnaround: Digital downloads are instant; editable printables typically 1–2 business days; custom timelines are quoted per project.
  • File formats: Canva links plus high-res PDF/PNG for templates; branding delivers logo files (PNG, PDF, source) and guidelines.
  • Revisions: Typically 1 round included (custom packages often 1–2); extra tweaks available if needed and may cost a little more.
  • Custom work: Yes—branding, Canva templates, party favors (digital or shipped), and special requests.

Shipping & Returns

Digital items deliver instantly. Physical/printed items are produced then shipped per listing; personalized items are non-refundable, and I'll fix any file issues right away.

Privacy: I only use your details to respond and deliver your designs.
