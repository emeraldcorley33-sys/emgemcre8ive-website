About Emerald

Designer, celebration enthusiast, and creator of joyful, intentional designs.

Hi, I'm Emerald.

I'm a designer who believes that every celebration, brand, and keepsake deserves to feel warm, polished, and intentional. Whether it's a birthday party, a brand refresh, or a personalized gift, I create designs that make moments memorable.

My approach

  • Clear communication: I listen to your vision and ask the right questions to bring it to life.
  • Fast turnarounds: Printables in 1-2 days, custom projects on a timeline that works for you.
  • Production-ready files: Everything ships print-ready so you can launch with confidence.
  • Strategy-led branding: Built for founders, marketers, and emerging brands who want their identity to stand out.

What I create

Branding suites with logos, color systems, and guidelines. Party packages for birthdays, baby showers, and holidays. Printables and keepsakes like wall décor, stickers, t‑shirts, personalized gifts, and memory pieces. Plus custom designs for anything unique and one‑of‑a‑kind.

Why I do this

I love helping people celebrate, launch businesses, and create keepsakes they'll treasure. Design isn't just about how something looks—it's about how it makes you feel. Every project I take on is a chance to add a little more joy and intention to someone's life.
